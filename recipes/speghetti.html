Odin's Pasta

The only simply dinner to make for multiple nights and multiple people. Pasta is so simple you can make a batch without thinking.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Butter
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Grated Parmasean Cheese

Directions

  1. Boil water and add noodles.
  2. While noodles boil slowly heat up Pasta Sauce.
  3. After noodles are done drain water.
  4. Add warmed pasta sauce.
  5. Serve with Parmasean Cheese.
