Odin's Mac n Cheese

Kraft Mac-n-Cheese. Straight from the box it's easy to make and easy to make for our family. Simple ingredients and no hassle.

Ingredients

  • Kraft Box MacnCheese
  • Milk
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Boil water.
  2. Add box noodles to boiling water.
  3. Boil for 9 minutes or until done.
  4. Drain noodles.
  5. Add butter and cheese packet.
  6. Add milk and mix until desired consitancy.
